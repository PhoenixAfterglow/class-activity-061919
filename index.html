He May Not Be "The Most Interesting Man in the World"

(But He Makes A Close 2nd)

Johnny Maravelis is the son of 2 immigrant parents. Both parents are Mediterranean. Johnny grew up in "Wilmington, MA. It is a boring town, filled with lots of grannies. You know it's generally a quiet town when the main retail stores includes, but not limits to, Savers, Dollar Tree, and yeah that's about it. (OK so maybe there's a Market Basket and 6 Dunkin Donuts).

It should be of no surprise that the youth of Wilmington, MA typically turn to drugs — marijuana to be exact. But, smoking herb wasn't a satisfying escape for Johnny, so he decided to hop on a plane to California. At the time, he only had $300 in his wallet. He had no job, no home, and wasn't enrolled into a college. Looking back, this is absolutely a crazy thing to do, now knowing that Los Angeles is the "homeless capitol of the world."
